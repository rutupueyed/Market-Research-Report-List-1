<p><p>The water jet cutter market is primarily categorized into three types: Pure Waterjet, Abrasive Jet, and Others. Pure Waterjet systems utilize high-pressure water streams for cutting softer materials like foam and rubber, providing a clean edge. Abrasive Jet systems mix water with abrasives to cut through harder materials such as metal and stone, enhancing cutting efficiency. The "Others" category includes specialized or hybrid systems that combine features from both types, catering to unique applications across various industries.</p></p>
