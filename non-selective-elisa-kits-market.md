<p><strong>The Non-Selective ELISA Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ELISA</li><li>Sandwich ELISA</li><li>Indirect ELISA</li><li>Competitive ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Selective ELISA Kits market includes several types of assays used for detecting and quantifying proteins or other biomolecules. Direct ELISA involves the attachment of the primary antibody to the target antigen, allowing for straightforward detection. Sandwich ELISA uses a two-antibody system to capture and detect the antigen, while Indirect ELISA employs a secondary antibody for enhanced signal amplification. Competitive ELISA measures the competition between the target antigen and a labeled antigen, offering sensitivity in quantification. Each type serves specific research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
