<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flow Wrap Equipment Market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal flow wrap machines package products in a horizontal plane, making them ideal for items like snacks, baked goods, and pharmaceuticals. They are efficient for high-speed production and easy integration into existing lines. In contrast, vertical flow wrap machines operate by feeding products vertically, which is suitable for packaging bulkier items like pouches or agricultural products. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the product characteristics and packaging requirements.</p></p>
