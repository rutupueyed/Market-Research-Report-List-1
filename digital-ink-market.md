<p><p>The digital ink market comprises several types based on the solvent used. Solvent-based inks are suitable for outdoor applications due to their durability and resistance to weathering. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, ideal for indoor printing but with limited outdoor durability. UV-cured inks offer quick drying and vibrant colors when exposed to UV light, making them suitable for high-speed printing. Additionally, other market types include dye-sublimation and eco-solvent inks, catering to specific requirements in various printing applications.</p></p>
