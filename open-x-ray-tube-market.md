<p><p>The Open X-ray Tube market is categorized into two primary types based on focal spot sizes: micro-focus X-ray tubes and nano-focus X-ray tubes. Micro-focus X-ray tubes feature focal spots ranging from 2 to 300 μm, offering higher resolution imaging for applications such as materials testing and medical diagnostics. Nano-focus X-ray tubes, with focal spots smaller than 2 μm, provide ultra-high resolution and precision, making them ideal for advanced research, semiconductor analysis, and detailed imaging in industrial applications.</p></p>
