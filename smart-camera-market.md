<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart camera market is primarily segmented into CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) technologies. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and smaller size, making them ideal for portable devices and applications requiring high integration. Conversely, CCD sensors offer superior image quality and lower noise, making them suitable for high-end photography and professional applications. These distinctions influence their adoption across consumer electronics, surveillance, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
