<p><ul><li>Disc brakes</li><li>Drum brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive high-performance brake system market primarily consists of disc brakes and drum brakes. Disc brakes utilize a flat rotor and caliper design, offering superior heat dissipation and stopping power, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, drum brakes feature a cylindrical design where brake shoes press outward against the drum, providing effective braking at a lower cost but less efficiency in high-stress conditions. Both systems are essential for vehicle safety and performance, catering to various automotive needs.</p></p>
