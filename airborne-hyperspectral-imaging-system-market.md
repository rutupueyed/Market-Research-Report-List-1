<p><ul><li>Visible/near-Infrared(VNIR)</li><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li><li>Others (UV-Vis, FT-IR, Combined technology, etc.)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airborne hyperspectral imaging system market is categorized into several types based on spectral range. Visible/Near-Infrared (VNIR) systems capture data in the visible light and near-infrared spectrum, ideal for agriculture and vegetation analysis. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) focuses on moisture content and mineral identification. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) detects thermal emissions, useful in surveillance and industrial applications. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) specializes in thermal imaging for environmental monitoring. Other technologies include UV-Vis for biological studies and FT-IR for material analysis, often incorporating combined technologies for enhanced capabilities.</p></p>
