<p><p>Hot runner systems are essential in injection molding, enhancing efficiency and quality. The valve gate hot runner market focuses on systems that use a valve to control the flow of molten plastic, allowing for precise timing and reduced waste. This type is ideal for intricate designs requiring consistent quality. In contrast, the open gate hot runner market involves systems without valves, providing simpler designs and lower costs but potentially leading to more material waste and reduced quality in complex applications.</p></p>
