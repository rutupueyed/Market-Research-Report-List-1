<p><strong>The Ureteral Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Pigtail Stents</li><li>Closed End Stents</li><li>Multiloop Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ureteral stents market is categorized into three main types: Double Pigtail Stents, Closed End Stents, and Multiloop Stents. Double Pigtail Stents feature coiled ends that prevent migration and ensure effective urine drainage, making them ideal for temporary use. Closed End Stents have a sealed distal end, often used in obstructive cases. Multiloop Stents incorporate multiple loops for enhanced flexibility and reduced encrustation, catering to specific clinical needs and providing longer-term solutions in ureteral obstruction management.</p></p>
