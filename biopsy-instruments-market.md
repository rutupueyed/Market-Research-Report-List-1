<p><strong>The Biopsy Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Needle Biopsy</li><li>Aspiration Biopsy</li><li>Vacuum Assisted Biopsy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopsy instruments market is categorized into three main types: Core Needle Biopsy, Aspiration Biopsy, and Vacuum Assisted Biopsy. Core Needle Biopsy utilizes a hollow needle to extract tissue samples, providing larger specimens for diagnosis. Aspiration Biopsy employs a fine needle to remove fluid or cells, typically for smaller lesions. Vacuum Assisted Biopsy combines suction with a rotating cutting mechanism, allowing multiple samples to be taken through a single incision, making it efficient for larger areas of interest.</p></p>
