<p><ul><li>Digital Signal Transmission</li><li>Analog Signal Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless guiding systems are categorized into two main types based on signal transmission methods: digital signal transmission and analog signal transmission. Digital signal transmission utilizes encoded data, providing enhanced clarity, reliability, and security for communication. This method is ideal for modern applications requiring precise direction and real-time updates. In contrast, analog signal transmission relies on continuous signals, which can be simpler but may experience interference, impacting the signal quality. Both types serve different needs within navigation and guiding applications in various industries.</p></p>
