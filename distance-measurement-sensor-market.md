<p><p>The distance measurement sensor market encompasses various technologies designed to determine the distance between objects. Inductive sensors sense metallic objects through electromagnetic fields. Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves for distance detection, and laser diode sensors provide precise measurements using laser beams. Photoelectric sensors detect light interruption, draw wire sensors measure distance via cable length, and image sensors analyze visual input for distance estimation, alongside other innovative types.</p></p>
