<p><strong>The Lake and Pond Dredging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Dredging</li><li>Mechanical Dredging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lake and pond dredging services are categorized into two main types: hydraulic dredging and mechanical dredging. Hydraulic dredging uses high-pressure water jets to suspend sediment, which is then pumped away, making it effective for softer materials and minimizing disturbance to the surrounding environment. In contrast, mechanical dredging employs heavy machinery, such as excavators or clamshells, to physically remove sediment and debris. This method is suitable for tougher materials and provides greater control over the dredging process.</p></p>
