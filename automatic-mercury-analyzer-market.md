<p><strong>The Automatic Mercury Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Mercury Analyzer market comprises two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzers. Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Analyzers measure mercury concentrations by vaporizing samples and using light absorption to quantify mercury levels. In contrast, Cold Atomic Fluorescence Analyzers detect mercury by exciting vaporized mercury atoms to emit light, which is then measured. Both technologies offer high sensitivity and accuracy for monitoring mercury in various environmental and industrial applications, ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>
