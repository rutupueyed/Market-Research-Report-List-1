<p><ul><li>Cross Type</li><li>Parallel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coupling filters are crucial components in telecommunications and power systems, categorized mainly into two market types: cross type and parallel type. Cross type filters are designed to minimize interference between closely spaced frequency bands, making them ideal for applications requiring precise frequency separation. In contrast, parallel type filters focus on enhancing signal integrity by providing higher impedance paths for unwanted frequencies, effectively filtering out noise. Both types play vital roles in ensuring optimal performance in various electronic systems.</p></p>
