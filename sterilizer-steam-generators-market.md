<p><strong>The Sterilizer Steam Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Boiler</li><li>Remote Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilizer steam generator market features two primary types: integral boilers and remote boilers. Integral boilers are compact units where the steam generation and sterilization processes occur in the same location, offering ease of installation and maintenance. In contrast, remote boilers are separate units that generate steam and can be located distant from the sterilization unit, providing flexibility in design and potentially serving multiple sterilizers. Each type caters to specific operational needs and facility layouts, influencing their adoption in various industries.</p></p>
