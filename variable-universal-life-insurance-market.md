<p><p>Variable Universal Life Insurance (VUL) encompasses two primary market types: Flexible Premium Universal Life Insurance and Fixed Premium Universal Life Insurance. Flexible Premium VUL allows policyholders to adjust premium amounts and timing, offering adaptability to financial situations. Conversely, Fixed Premium VUL requires set premium payments at regular intervals, providing stability and predictability in investment growth. Both types combine life insurance coverage with cash value accumulation, allowing policyholders to invest in various options, affecting the policy's overall performance and benefits.</p></p>
