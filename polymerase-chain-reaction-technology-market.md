<p><strong>The Polymerase Chain Reaction Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Polymerase Chain Reaction</li><li>Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) technology market is primarily categorized into two types: Digital Polymerase Chain Reaction (dPCR) and Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction (qPCR). dPCR offers precise quantification of nucleic acids, enabling detection of rare variants and facilitating applications in clinical diagnostics and research. In contrast, qPCR allows for the monitoring of DNA amplification in real-time, making it suitable for various applications, including gene expression analysis and pathogen detection. Both technologies are essential for advancements in molecular biology and genetic testing.</p></p>
