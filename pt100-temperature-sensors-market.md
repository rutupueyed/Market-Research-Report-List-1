<p><strong>The Pt100 Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-wire Pt100 Temperature Sensors</li><li>3-wire Pt100 Temperature Sensors</li><li>4-wire Pt100 Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pt100 temperature sensor market includes three primary types: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire sensors. 2-wire Pt100 sensors are simple and cost-effective but may experience measurement errors due to lead resistance. 3-wire sensors improve accuracy by using a third wire to compensate for lead resistance, making them more suitable for industrial applications. 4-wire Pt100 sensors offer the highest precision by entirely eliminating lead resistance effects, making them ideal for critical temperature measurements in laboratory and process control settings.</p></p>
