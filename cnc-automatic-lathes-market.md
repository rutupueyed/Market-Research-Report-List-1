<p><p>The CNC automatic lathe market is primarily categorized into horizontal and vertical lathes. Horizontal lathes, which are commonly used for machining cylindrical parts, feature a spindle positioned parallel to the ground, allowing for easier access and tool changes. Conversely, vertical lathes have a spindle oriented vertically, suitable for larger workpieces and producing more complex geometries. Both types leverage CNC technology to enhance precision, efficiency, and automation in manufacturing, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
