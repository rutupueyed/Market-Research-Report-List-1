<p><p>Electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are critical in OLED manufacturing for holding substrates during processing. The Coulomb type utilizes electrostatic force generated by charged plates, allowing for stable wafer positioning. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type employs a dielectric layer to enhance adhesion through induced charges, making it efficient for various substrate materials. Both types ensure precise alignment and improved yield in OLED production, catering to different operational needs based on material properties and processing conditions.</p></p>
