<p><p>The recycled rubber flooring market is primarily divided into non-interlock and interlock flooring types. Non-interlock flooring offers a seamless appearance and is ideal for large areas, providing stability and easy maintenance. In contrast, interlock flooring consists of modular tiles that interconnect, allowing for easier installation, customization, and flexibility in design. Both types leverage the durability and sustainability of recycled rubber, making them popular choices for gyms, playgrounds, and various commercial spaces, enhancing safety and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
