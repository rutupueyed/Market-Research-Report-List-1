<p><p>The Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) market encompasses various manufacturing techniques. Laser Direct Structuring (LDS) involves using laser technology to create intricate, three-dimensional electrical circuits on molded plastic substrates, enhancing design flexibility. Two-Shot Molding combines two materials in a single mold to integrate electrical components seamlessly within an enclosure, optimizing functionality and space. Other methods include traditional injection molding and hybrid techniques, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, promoting innovation and efficiency.</p></p>
