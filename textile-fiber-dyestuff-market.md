<p><p>The Textile Fiber Dyestuff market comprises various dye types, each catering to specific fibers and applications. Disperse dyes are primarily used for synthetic fibers, offering vibrant colors and excellent fastness. Reactive dyes bond chemically with fibers, suitable for cellulosic materials. Sulfur dyes provide bright shades and high wash fastness, commonly used for denim. Vat dyes are known for their color stability and are ideal for cotton. Acid dyes excel on wool and nylon, while other dyes encompass specialized options for diverse textiles.</p></p>
