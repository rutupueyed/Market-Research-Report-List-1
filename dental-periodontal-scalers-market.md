<p><strong>The Dental Periodontal Scalers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Dental Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Dental Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental periodontal scalers market is primarily categorized into two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric scalers. Magnetostrictive scalers utilize a magnetic field to produce vibrations in vibrating tips, effectively removing plaque and calculus. They offer a broader range of motion and can operate at various frequencies. Piezoelectric scalers, on the other hand, rely on ultrasonic vibrations generated by electrical signals, allowing for precise and controlled scaling with less heat generation. Each type is essential for optimal periodontal health and dental hygiene.</p></p>
