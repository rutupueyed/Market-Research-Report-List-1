<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resonance</li><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Radio Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charger transmitter market is primarily categorized into three types: magnetic resonance, electromagnetic induction, and radio frequency. </p><p>Magnetic resonance uses magnetic fields to transfer energy over short distances, allowing for efficient charging of compatible devices. Electromagnetic induction relies on inductive coupling between coils to charge devices, requiring close proximity. Radio frequency charging employs electromagnetic waves to transfer energy over longer distances, enabling charging without direct contact. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various consumer preferences and technological requirements.</p></p>
