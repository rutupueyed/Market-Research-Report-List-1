<p><p>The Pipeline Pigging Services market encompasses various types of pigging techniques used for inspecting and maintaining pipelines. Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) pigging detects wall defects through magnetic fields, while Ultrasonic Test pigging uses sound waves for assessing pipe integrity. Utility pigging focuses on cleaning and maintaining flow, whereas Caliper pigging measures pipe diameter and detects deformations. Each method plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety, efficiency, and reliability of pipeline systems across various industries.</p></p>
