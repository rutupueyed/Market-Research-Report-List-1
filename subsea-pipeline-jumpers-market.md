<p><ul><li>Rigid Pipeline Jumpers</li><li>Flexible Pipeline Jumpers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea pipeline jumpers market consists of two primary types: rigid pipeline jumpers and flexible pipeline jumpers. Rigid pipeline jumpers are characterized by their fixed structure, designed for specific applications where stability and robust design are essential, often used in deep-water projects. In contrast, flexible pipeline jumpers are constructed from materials that allow for movement and adaptability, making them suitable for dynamic environments and areas with variable seabed conditions, enhancing installation versatility and mitigating stress on pipelines.</p></p>
