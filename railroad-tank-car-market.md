<p><p>The railroad tank car market includes various types catering to different transportation needs. DOT-111 cars are versatile, used for hazardous materials but lack robust safety features. AAR-211 is a legacy design focusing on smaller loads. DOT-112 cars are optimized for transporting liquefied gases, while DOT-105 cars are built for high-pressure transport of products like propane. DOT-117 represents the latest safety enhancements, designed for transporting flammable liquids, featuring improved puncture resistance and enhanced protection, addressing regulatory changes and safety concerns.</p></p>
