<p><ul><li>Single-channel</li><li>Multiple-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorcycle Brake By Wire (BBW) systems can be categorized into single-channel and multiple-channel markets. Single-channel systems use one input to control the brakes, typically suitable for simpler applications or lower-performance motorcycles, offering cost-effective solutions. In contrast, multiple-channel systems provide independent control over each brake, enhancing performance, safety, and stability. This complexity allows for advanced features like cornering brake assistance and traction control, making multiple-channel systems popular in high-performance and luxury motorcycles where safety and rider experience are paramount.</p></p>
