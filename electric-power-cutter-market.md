<p><p>The electric power cutter market can be divided into two main types: corded and battery-operated cutters. Corded cutters provide continuous power and are typically more powerful, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks. However, they require proximity to power outlets, limiting mobility. On the other hand, battery-operated cutters offer greater portability and convenience, allowing for use in remote areas without power access. While their cutting power may be less than corded models, advancements in battery technology are enhancing their performance and runtime.</p></p>
