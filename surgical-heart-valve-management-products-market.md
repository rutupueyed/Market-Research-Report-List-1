Heart valve surgery refers to traditional surgical methods to repair or replace malfunctioning valves. Heart valve repair focuses on reconstructing the existing valve to improve function while preserving native tissue. Heart valve replacement involves surgically removing a diseased valve and replacing it with a mechanical or biological prosthetic valve, ensuring normal blood flow.</p></p>
