<p><p>The Automatic Wafer Debonder market includes various methods for detaching wafers from substrates. Thermal debonding utilizes heat to soften adhesive bonds, facilitating easy separation. Mechanical debonding employs physical forces, such as pressure or vibration, to achieve wafer detachment. Laser debonding leverages focused laser beams to selectively break the adhesive bonds at a molecular level, ensuring precision. Additionally, "Others" encompass emerging or niche techniques that may utilize innovative technologies or hybrid methods to enhance wafer debonding efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
